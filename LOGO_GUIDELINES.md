# HabitForge Branding & Logo Guidelines

HabitForge is designed to feel like a premium, top-tier SaaS product. Our brand identity revolves around the concept of "Forging" — taking raw potential and striking it consistently to build something strong, enduring, and beautiful.

## Logo Metaphor
Our logo combines the **Flame** (representing passion, energy, and streaks) and the **Anvil** (representing consistency, hard work, and the forge). 
The flat, vector-style design ensures it scales perfectly from a tiny favicon to a massive App Store feature graphic.

## Brand Colors

We use a carefully curated dual-tone primary palette:

### Primary: Deep Indigo
- **Hex**: `#4F46E5`
- **Usage**: Primary brand color, app header backgrounds, primary CTA buttons, splash screen backgrounds.
- **Why**: Indigo conveys trust, focus, and modern technology.

### Secondary: Emerald Green
- **Hex**: `#10B981`
- **Usage**: Success states, completion rings, 100% weekly charts.
- **Why**: Green is the universal color of success, growth, and forward momentum.

### Accent: Forging Orange
- **Hex**: `#F97316`
- **Usage**: Streaks, the flame in the logo, "hot" activity in the heatmap.
- **Why**: Represents the heat of the forge and the energy of maintaining a streak.

## Typography
HabitForge uses clean, geometric sans-serif typography. While the app uses system fonts for performance (San Francisco on iOS, Roboto on Android), all marketing assets should use **Inter** or **Outfit** for a sleek, modern look.

## Usage Rules
- **Spacing**: Always leave clear space around the logo equal to 25% of the logo's width.
- **Backgrounds**: The logo looks best on deep, dark backgrounds (like our deep indigo). When used on light backgrounds, ensure the anvil is dark enough to maintain contrast.
- **No Stretching**: Never stretch or distort the logo aspect ratio.
